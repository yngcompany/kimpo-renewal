Jeollyuri Port is a fi shing village located on the northernmost point of the Hangang River.
Visitors can taste various seasonal fi sh dishes and purchase fi sh at low prices directly from the fi shermen at local fi sh markets.
Prawn and Gizzard Shad Festivals are held in autumn.
